Products and Services
Save Save Clear Slate

Products and services are the part of your business that you, as an artist, should feel most excited about. These are the ways you share your creativity with the world. This section should be the most fun! Think about all the things you offer. Do you mostly have physical art that you sell? Is your gift your time and energy? Maybe you do a little bit of both, like sell prints but do workshops monthly. It may be a little strange for you to think about what you're "selling," but the reality is that you're offering something that people will pay for in a number of ways (love, skillshare and $$ cash money) and that's what's going to sustain you.

Overview

Give us a general idea of what you're offering. What is your gift to the world? Are they products (tangible) or services (time-based)?

Importance

Imagine you're at a gallery/music vanue checking out for your favorite artist. What do you tell your girlfiend how they made you feel? What is so awesome about them that you feel they are worth supporting? Now think about what value you offer others.

Products

Do you sell drawings, mixtapes, posters, chapbooks or something that I can physically touch? Or is it a digital product like an e-book, website or a video? These are products too. If you create something and can walk away from it and it stil exists, then you're creating products. List all your products with a name and a description.
